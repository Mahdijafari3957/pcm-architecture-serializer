The Healthcare Management System is an appointment management platform for coordinating patient appointments and providing role-based access to patient-related information. At its core, the system includes an Appointment Management component that handles appointment scheduling and interacts with a Patient DB Management component to retrieve relevant patient data. The system serves three primary user roles: patient, physician, and receptionist, each through a dedicated user interface component. Access to information is restricted according to role. Patients can view their own complete information, physicians can access treatment-related medical details and special patient needs, and receptionists are limited to administrative and scheduling information without access to confidential health data.
