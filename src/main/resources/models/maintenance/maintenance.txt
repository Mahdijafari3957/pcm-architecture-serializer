The scenario involves two organizations: a manufacturer (M) that owns and operates production machines, 
and a service contractor (S) responsible for maintaining and repairing those machines. 
During normal operation, an S technician can perform maintenance via a terminal, 
while the machine stores its data on an external storage system. 
However, the technician must not be allowed to access the machine’s log data under normal conditions, 
since the logs may contain sensitive information (e.g., employee names at M or detailed operational data). 
In the event of a machine failure, an S technician is permitted limited access to the log data for troubleshooting.
